Who I Am

Hi, I’m Abhishek — creator of Career Journey / The Abhi Show. I interview real people from different backgrounds to capture and share their career journeys, struggles, achievements, experiences, and practical learnings.

I started this platform because I believe people should learn not only from textbooks but also from real-life experiences and informative content that can guide them in making better career and life decisions.

🚀 Mission of the Platform

To build a platform that shares authentic career stories, valuable experiences, and informative content that educates, motivates, and helps the next generation grow with the right knowledge and inspiration.
